About Me

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering student at Eastern University, Bangladesh, with a focus on machine learning, algorithms, and brain-computer interface (BCI) security. Driven by curiosity about how the human mind and digital systems intersect, I am drawn to exploring the intersection of neuroscience, computing, and cybersecurity, with a focus on safeguarding neural data from emerging threats. Alongside this, I serve as the Director of Human Resources at International Youth Change Makers (IYCM), an international youth organization based in Bangladesh, where I contribute to leadership development and youth engagement. In this role, I support a global network of over 50,000 young leaders by contributing to strategic coordination, strengthening organizational capacity, and advancing youth empowerment initiatives. My goal is to bridge the gap between advanced technology and impactful social leadership, contributing to a more secure and empowered future.
